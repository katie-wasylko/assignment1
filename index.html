The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band, started in Liverpool, England in 1960. They were, and still are, one of the most successful and influential bands in the history of modern music. The Beatles used parts of many music genres through their career, including classical, psychedelic rock and pop music.

I say in speeches that a plausible mission of artists is to make people appreciate being alive at least a little bit. I am then asked if I know of any artists who pulled that off. I reply, 'The Beatles did.-Kurt Vonnegut

Brian Epstein was their manager. George Martin produced all of their albums and singles except for the album “Let It Be”, which was produced by the famous 1960s producer Phil Spector. Almost all of their music was recorded at Abbey Road Studios in London.

Members

  • John Lennon (rhythm guitar, keyboards)
  • Paul McCartney (bass guitar, keyboards
  • George Harrison (lead singer, sitar)
  • Ringo Star (drums, percussion)
